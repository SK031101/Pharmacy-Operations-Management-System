# Pharmacy Operations Management System

A MERN-based Pharmacy Operations Management System designed to streamline inventory management, billing, stock monitoring, and employee workflows for pharmacy businesses.

## Problem Statement

Many pharmacy stores still rely on manual record-keeping, paper registers, and employee-dependent workflows. This creates several operational challenges:

* Difficulty tracking medicine inventory accurately
* Stock mismatches and inventory leakage
* Manual billing and record management
* Cash tracking issues
* Medicine expiry monitoring challenges
* Long onboarding time for new employees
* Dependency on experienced staff for medicine locations and operational knowledge
* Lack of actionable business insights and analytics

This project aims to address these challenges through a centralized digital platform.

---

## Project Objective

The goal is to build a simple, scalable, and user-friendly system that helps pharmacy owners:

* Manage inventory efficiently
* Track stock movement
* Monitor medicine expiry dates
* Standardize workflows
* Improve employee productivity
* Reduce operational losses
* Generate business insights
